The Best Movies According to Ada

My top 3 all-time favourite movies:

1. Pulp Fiction (1994)

Directed by Quentin Tarantino

Main Charactes:

  • Vincent Vega
  • Jules Winnfield
  • Mia Wallace
  • Butch Coolidge

    • Quentin Tarantino's masterpiece that intertwines multiple storylines in a non-linear fashion, showcasing the lives of hitmen, a boxer, and a gangster's wife.

    2. Ethernal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (2004)

    Directed by Michel Gondry

    Main Charactes and actors:

  • Joel Barish (played by Jim Carrey)
  • Clementine Kruczynski (played by Kate Winslet)
  • Dr. Howard Mierzwiak (played by Tom Wilkinson)
  • Patrick (played by Elijah Wood)

    • A mind-bending romantic drama that delves into the complexities of love and memory, following a couple who undergo a procedure to erase each other from their minds.

    3. The Worst Person in the World (2021)

    Directed by Joachim Trier

    Main Charactes and actors:

  • Julie (played by Renate Reinsve)
  • Aksel (played by Anders Danielsen Lie)

    • A dark comedy that explores the life of a self-absorbed writer who navigates relationships and personal crises, ultimately questioning her own humanity.
