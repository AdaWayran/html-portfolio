About Me

My Developer Journey

Hi, I'm Ada Wayran, an aspiring Web Developer and Graphic Designer.

I'm passionate about creating visually striking and meaningful designs that communicate effectively.

I began my developer journey knowing one thing for sure: I wanted to be part of the fast-moving world of technology and the endless opportunities that come with it. I'm passionate about learning new skills and using my creativity to communicate through visually striking and meaningful designs.

While working full-time in nature research, I started learning on the side—watching YouTube channels by experienced developers and graphic designers, and exploring free resources like FreeCodeCamp to pick up the basics of coding. As I gained more confidence, I took things further with online courses on Udemy. One course that truly jumpstarted my journey was the Full-Stack Development Bootcamp by Dr. Angela Yu. It opened the door to a deeper understanding of web development and fueled my excitement even more.

I can’t wait to see how this skillset will evolve and where it will take me next!
